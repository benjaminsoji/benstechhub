Modular CSS: Why It Matters

By Ben • August 14, 2025

Modular CSS is a design philosophy that breaks your styles into reusable, maintainable chunks. Instead of one massive stylesheet, you organize styles by purpose: base, layout, components, and themes.

This approach improves scalability, reduces conflicts, and makes collaboration easier. Whether you're building a personal site or a large-scale app, modular CSS helps future-proof your codebase.

In this post, we’ll explore how Ben’s Tech Hub uses modular CSS to stay flexible and consistent across pages.
